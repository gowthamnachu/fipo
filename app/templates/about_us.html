About Fipo

Advanced Portfolio Optimization & Financial Analytics

Our Mission

Fipo is a cutting-edge portfolio optimization platform designed to help investors build better portfolios through advanced financial algorithms and intuitive visualizations.

Our mission is to democratize access to sophisticated portfolio optimization techniques that were previously only available to institutional investors and wealth managers.

Financial Terms & Definitions

Portfolio Optimization

The process of selecting the optimal allocation of assets to maximize returns while minimizing risk.

Asset Allocation

Strategic distribution of investments across various asset classes.

Sharpe Ratio

Measure of risk-adjusted return relative to the risk-free rate.

Volatility

Statistical measure of price variation and market uncertainty.

Momentum

Continuance of existing price trends in the market.

Moving Average

Technical indicator showing average price over time period.

Market Depth

Real-time display of buy and sell orders at various price levels in the market.

Intraday Trading

Buying and selling securities within the same trading day.

Fibonacci Retracement

Technical analysis tool using Fibonacci ratios to identify potential support/resistance levels.

Candlestick Pattern

Visual representation of price movements used in technical analysis to predict market trends.
